Archie is a sharp, creative, lighthearted, fun individual who likes to work hard, smart and all with a geeky personality. He puts in efforts and thought to give back to his community while pushing himself academically and all while participating in extracurricular activities at school. He pursues his passions (technology & community give-back) extensively by going above and beyond the call of duty, an example of the same is, completing two computer science pathways instead of one. He does this through a signature program called AIOT (Academy of Information Technology) that teaches students practical computer science/IT skills. He pursues his passion in any available personal time by learning programming languages (e.g. Python), Azure, and mobile app development to expand his scope of knowledge. Additionally, Archie was so good in programming skills that he got recommended by his teacher to a higher-level class. His extracurricular activities are aligned to his passion for technology too. To that end he helped establish a TSA (Technology Student Association) club and is an active member & parliamentarian of this club. He won in the top 3 at the state level in TSA competitions such as robotics, geo-spatial technology, and videogame development. He was also a national level participant in Cybersecurity. More importantly he is always eager to learn, he goes to his programming teacher frequently during lunch to work on projects and to ask additional clarification questions to satisfy his curiosity. More impressively, he also has an industry certification, CompTIA A+ (1001, 1002), which proves his ability to be successful in the industry and gives him an edge over his peers. Furthermore, he gives back to his community whether at school or across the nation. For example, he gave back over four times the required community service hours. He has volunteered at the Board of Elections and received an ‘Global Citizenship’ award of recognition because of his efforts & contributions to assist the voters and election commission staff for the successful conclusion of the early voting during the primaries. At such an early age, he has secured an internship which shows his proactiveness in learning and using his skills. This isn’t just any internship but supports his ethos of community give back. He empowers the labor unions nation-wide by assisting in the development of a cloud-based union membership management platform for them. Lastly, he has held many leadership positions in his school including Patriot Ambassador and being a club officer in TSA and has made numerous positive contributions to his school; for example, he was a graduation speaker for AOIT’s graduation and volunteered over the summer to help run the main-office. Last but not least he is in the Student Ambassadors and SGA, this shows his eagerness to lead, to make a difference wherever he can, and to be a positive influence on others. He is also the President Elect of the TSA club. This is an example of being a leader not a follower in order to make positive contributions and differences in his close community by being a role model to others. He is a good team player & a leader who likes to get everyone involved.
